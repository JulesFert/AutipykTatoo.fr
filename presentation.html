Biography
Interested in art since childhood, Sacha Boussières, under the pseudonyms of Sacha DesBois and Autipyk Tattoo, shares his creations on the Internet for many years. Born in august 2002, in France. He creates using various techniques but his preferred tools are charcoal, watercolor, ink and pyrography. Xe also enjoys crafting, sewing, drum embroidery, sculpting using clay, making pins and jewelry creation. Xe uses art as an outlet and also an alternative way of expressing xemself, resulting in a diverse and wide universe which is simultaneously colorful, dreamlike, fantastic, poetic and dark, gloomy, disturbing, gore.. Ve also employs art to bring awareness to many subjects he takes to heart including mental health awareness, autism acceptance, LGBTQIA+ pride... And many others.
